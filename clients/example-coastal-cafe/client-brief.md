## Business Description

The Saltwater Kitchen is a contemporary coastal cafe and restaurant on Mooloolaba Esplanade, offering locally-sourced breakfast, lunch, and dinner with panoramic ocean views. Known for their fresh seafood, seasonal menus, and relaxed coastal atmosphere that appeals to both locals and visitors seeking quality dining without pretension.

---

## Target Market

### Primary Audience
- **Demographics:** Adults 30-55, household income $100k+, couples and families
- **Psychographics:** Food-focused, appreciate quality ingredients, environmentally conscious, prefer experiences over possessions
- **Location:** 60% Sunshine Coast locals, 40% tourists/visitors

### Secondary Audience
- **Demographics:** 25-35 young professionals, social diners
- **Psychographics:** Instagram-active, brunch culture, value aesthetics

### Customer Pain Points
1. Finding restaurants that aren't tourist traps with inflated prices
2. Wanting ocean views without sacrificing food quality
3. Difficulty finding genuinely local/sustainable options

### Customer Desires
1. Memorable dining experience with beautiful setting
2. Fresh, seasonal food that celebrates coastal produce
3. Relaxed atmosphere suitable for special occasions and casual meals

---

## Brand Voice

### Tone
Warm, Fresh, Approachable

### Personality
Welcoming, locally-rooted, quality-focused, unpretentious, coastal

### Voice Examples
**They would say:**
- "Fresh from the Mooloolaba trawlers this morning"
- "We've partnered with local Sunshine Coast farms"
- "Pull up a chair and watch the waves"

**They would NOT say:**
- "Fine dining experience"
- "Exclusive beachfront location"
- "Award-winning celebrity chef"

### Keywords to Use
- Fresh, local, seasonal
- Ocean view, waterfront
- Coastal, Sunshine Coast
- Sustainable, local producers
- Relaxed, welcoming

### Keywords to Avoid
- Fine dining, exclusive, luxury
- Best, top-rated, award-winning (unless verified)
- Tourist, vacation, holiday
- Cheap, budget, affordable
